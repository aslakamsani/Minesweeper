How to Play Minesweeper

Objective: Clear the screen of mines by flagging all mined cells.

Cells: Left-clicking on a cell will reveal it, as well as all safe cells surrounding.

           If you click on a mine, you lose the game. Cells will be blank, mined, or will be

           filled with a number signifying the number of mines that directly surround that cell.

           For instance:

           - - -

           - 2 -

           - - -

           means that there are two mines in the vicinity.

Notes to Self: By right-clicking on a cell, you can cycle between a flag,

                       signifying that there is a mine on that cell, and a question mark,

                       signifying that you are unsure of the safety of the cell in question, or a blank cell.
